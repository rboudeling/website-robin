My favourite Projects

Photoshop

My favourite thing to do

I spend a lot of my free time making photoshop composites. Some are based off of tutorials from other artists, which I follow in order to improve my own skills. However, I most enjoy using my own imagination to create new images. Aside from Photoshop, I am also developing my skills in Illustrator and Blender. Progress in those programs coming soon!

Coding

A useful asset to have

I am not bad at coding, but definitely enjoy the creative side to it more. For example, I was far more passionate about creating the css of this website than building the body of it in html (although I can do that as well!). Furthermore, I have done some small projects in Processing. Click to see more about those.

Adobe XD App

My Own Personal App!

As part of a school assignment, I created an Adobe XD app of myself. I used various techniques in order to create an app which worked well. It is designed for mobile and contains features such as a Parallax. I certainly enjoy the visual design aspects which Adobe XD offers. Click to see more.

Research

Investigating what could go better!

Although research is not my favourite part of UX, it is very rewarding when you start to see new insights. I have also partaken in user trialling for the rijksstudio website. This was part of a group project in which my group members and I each had to facillitate a user navigating through the rijksstudio.nl website. Click to download the paper.
