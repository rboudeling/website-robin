About me

UX designer

With my creativity I always seek the boundaries of what is possible and will go to the utmost to achieve that. My passion lies in digital creativity whether this be art or coding. I always put my team before myself although I will always argue my points of view and indicate when I disagree with someone so we can find a constructive solution. With my creativity I would like to make innovative products to help the environment. My goal would be to participate in the development of a carbon-negative city. At the moment, I am focused on digital design. Going to school in different cultural environments and living in different countries has taught me to be adaptable and has shaped me to be internationally minded.
